Diversifying Your Portfolio: When you diversify, you aim to lower your risk by spreading out your investments. Some ways to diversify include investing in different classes or company sectors.
The Reality of Investment Risk: All investments carry some degree of risk. Stocks, bonds, mutual funds and exchange-traded funds can lose value, even all their value, if market conditions sour.
Evaluating Investment Performance To assess how well your investments are doing, you'll need to consider several different ways of measuring performance. The measures you choose will depend on the information you're looking for and the types of investments you own.
