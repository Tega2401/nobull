What should I invest in?


If you were to google what companies you should invest in so that it can be the next Amazon or Apple, most of the time, the companies experts tell you to invest in may not experience as much growth as before. If you want to find the next Amazon, look at companies in the following sectors: 5G, Cybersecurity, Data Analytics, AI, and Fintech. It is important to find companies where in 5 to 10 years other investors can build "castles-in-the-air".
